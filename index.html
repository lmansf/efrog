Meet efrog

Record or upload frog calls and get an instant AI-powered species identification. Currently covers cane toad, oak toad, and southern toad — with more on the way.

🌿

Why frogs matter

Amphibians are disappearing faster than almost any other animal group. Their presence — or absence — is one of the earliest signals of ecosystem stress.

📡

You're doing science

Every recording you submit is logged to a real research dataset. Patterns in where and when species appear help scientists track range shifts and population changes.

🎙️

Getting the best results

Record 5–10 seconds near water at dusk or dawn. The closer you are to the calls, the better. Sign in to build a personal history and contribute accuracy feedback.
